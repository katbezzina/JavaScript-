Fruitylicious

Tired of eating plain fruit everyday?

Here you can find creative breakfast, dessert or snack recipes using some of the most nutricious fresh fruit: apples, strawberries, banana, blueberries, kiwi, oranges and/or lemons to make sure that you will always find something to do with those extra oranges sitting on your kitchen counter for too long! ...

This is also a great way to introduce fruit to children or adults who are not big fans of eating fruit. The vitamins, minerals andantioxidants most fruit contain are vital so such recipes are definitely worth a try.
