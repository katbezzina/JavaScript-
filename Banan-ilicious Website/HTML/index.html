Fruitylicious

This webpage is designed to help you find creative breakfast, dessert or snack recipes using fresh fruit. Fruits are an excellent source of essential vitamins and minerals, and they are high in fiber.

Tired of eating raw fruit everyday? Why not combine them in an easy, creative way to your daily meals instead? ...

This is also a great way to introduce fruit to children or adults who are not big fans of eating fruit. The antioxidants most fruit contain are vital so such recipes are definitely worth a try!
